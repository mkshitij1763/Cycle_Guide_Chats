    What is the difference or mean by Ovulation confirmed vs Ovulation Day
----------------------------------------

[10:06 pm] Coach:
    Hey, Ovulation Day refers to the day you likely ovulated. LH is the hormone that triggers ovulation, and its rise above baseline is marked as peak fertility. Since ovulation typically happens 24–36 hours after the LH surge, Inito considers the day after peak fertility as your ovulation day for ease of calculation.
    
    Ovulation Confirmed, on the other hand, means that your body has actually ovulated. After the egg is released, the follicle becomes the corpus luteum, which produces progesterone. PdG (the urine metabolite of progesterone) rises as a result, so a steady and consistent rise in PdG after the LH surge confirms that ovulation has occurred.
