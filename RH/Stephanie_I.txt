    If my progesterone drops, but it is telling me it’s still waiting for it to rise, did I most likely not ovulate?
----------------------------------------

[10:31 pm] Coach:
    Hey Stephanie, to confirm ovulation with Inito, we look for a steady and consistent rise in PdG from your baseline over a few days. PdG fluctuations are typical in luteal phase and it can take a little time to show a consistent rise after your LH surge. PdG usually starts rising shortly after LH surge and typically peaks around 6–8 DPO, but in some cases, confirmation might take up to 9 days. If you would like to know more, this blog might be helpful- https://blog.inito.com/progesterone-after-ovulation/
