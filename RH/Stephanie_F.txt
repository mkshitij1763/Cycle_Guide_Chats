[01:04 am] Coach:
    Here’s why Inito is saying 10 DPO:
    You are at 10 DPO because your LH rose on CD 34 and you got your 2nd Peak Fertility (PF) reading.
    During that surge, your LH continued to rise in more of a plateau-type surge. When LH behaves like that, it becomes difficult to pinpoint the exact ovulation timing.
    
    Here’s how DPO is calculated:
    DPO starts after your LH surge, once you get Ovulation Confirmed (OC).
    For ease of calculation, Inito marks ovulation as the day after your LH surge.
    Biologically, ovulation typically occurs:
    24–36 hours after the LH rise
    8–20 hours after your LH peak
    
    With this kind of surge pattern, the only true way to confirm which day you ovulated is:
    Waiting for your period, or
    Getting a positive pregnancy test
    If you would like to know more this blog might be useful- 
    https://blog.inito.com/lh-rise-vs-lh-surge-vs-peak-fertility/
