[09:57 am] Coach:
    Hey Emily, hormone levels can vary a lot from person to person and Inito focuses on your hormone trends. While PdG is helpful for confirming ovulation, you need to focus on trends rather than values alone. That said, Inito measures hormone metabolites in urine, while blood tests measure the hormone levels in your bloodstream. Inito cannot be used to diagnose low PdG levels so, if you feel your levels are too low, I'd recommend to get a blood test done and reach out to your healthcare provider.
