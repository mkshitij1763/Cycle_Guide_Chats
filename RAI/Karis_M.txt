[07:58 pm] Coach:
    Inito measures your PdG (urine metabolite of progesterone) in urine. 
    Progesterone is released in pulses and can typically fluctuate throughout the day.
    Apart from that, how much of progesterone gets metabolized and excreted in urine is variable as well.
    Your hormones fluctuate due to diet, exercise, stress, and water intake.
    
    Hence the values would vary throughout every cycle.
    Instead of focusing on standalone values, focus on the hormone trends throughout your cycle.

[12:25 am] Karis M:
    Is it normal to still get a negative pregnancy test at 11dpo
----------------------------------------

[12:28 am] Coach:
    A positive test can show up anywhere from ~9 DPO to ~14 DPO because:
    
    • Implantation timing varies: hCG only starts after the embryo implants, which can happen earlier for some people and a few days later for others.
    • hCG rises at different speeds: even after implantation, it can take time to reach the level a urine test can detect.
    • Test + urine factors: some tests are more sensitive, and first morning urine is usually more concentrated.
    
    ✨ Best tip: If you get a negative at 9–12 DPO, it doesn’t rule anything out. Testing closer to 13–14 DPO (or on/after your expected period) is usually the most reliable.
