[08:16 am] Coach:
    With Inito, ovulation is confirmed when PdG (urine metabolite of progsterone) rises and stays elevated after the LH surge.
    
    Typical timeline
    LH surge: signals ovulation will occur in ~24–36 hours
    Ovulation: usually within 24–36 hours after the surge or 8-20 hours from the peak
    PdG rise begins: a few days after the LH surge and peaks at 6-8 DPO
    Ovulation confirmed: after a sustained PdG rise 
    
    👉 Most users see confirmation about 5 days after the LH surge.
    
    Why the wait?
    After the egg is released, the follicle becomes the corpus luteum, which produces progesterone. PdG rises only once this progesterone production is established and sustained.
    
    What if PdG doesn’t rise right away?
    Small delays can happen. Continue testing for a few more days to capture a consistent rise.
